EmoLab-AI

Advancing Multimodal Understanding and Perception

EmoLab-AI is a research group dedicated to exploring how machines can perceive and understand the world as humans do — particularly through multimodal information such as vision, speech, and biomedical signals. Our mission is to empower machines with the ability to learn from diverse modalities, with the ultimate goal of making meaningful contributions to both the scientific community and society at large.
